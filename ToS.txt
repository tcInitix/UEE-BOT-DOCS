2. DESCRIPTION OF SERVICE

The Bot provides various community management and utility features for The 
Coalition Discord server, including but not limited to:

  • Slash commands: /banner, /divisions, /feedback, /org, /pvp, /rank, /ranks, 
    /register, /tracker, /say, and /dm
  • Support ticket system (HelpDesk, Reports & Complaints, Suggestions, Staff 
    Applications) with automatic transcript generation
  • Division role selection panels
  • XP and leveling system that grants Discord roles based on server activity
  • Attributed and anonymous feedback systems
  • RSI (Roberts Space Industries) verification to confirm TCICORPS 
    organization membership
  • Logging of message edits, deletions, and member join/leave events to 
    staff-only channels
  • Monitoring of thecoalition.gg and related public endpoints
  • Image generation (banners, division cards) using user avatars and display 
    names
  • PVP kill/loss tracking and other Star Citizen-themed utilities

profile you provide to verify:

By using this feature, you confirm that the RSI handle belongs to you and 
consent to this public-page lookup. We do not log into RSI or collect your 
credentials.
