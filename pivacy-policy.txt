UEE BOT PRIVACY POLICY
Last Updated: June 19, 2026

================================================================================

1. INTRODUCTION

This Privacy Policy explains how The Coalition / TCICORPS ("we," "us," or "the 
Operator") collects, uses, stores, and protects information when you use the 
UEE Bot (the "Bot") on The Coalition Discord server.

By using the Bot, you consent to the practices described in this Privacy Policy. 
This policy supplements our Terms of Service and Discord's Privacy Policy. If 
you do not agree with this policy, please do not use the Bot.

================================================================================

2. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

We collect the following types of information:

Discord Account Information:
  • User ID, username, display name, avatar, and roles

Usage and Activity Data:
  • Messages in monitored channels (for XP calculation and moderation)
  • Interactions with Bot commands (e.g., /feedback, /register, /pvp)
  • Ticket content and transcripts
  • Feedback submissions (named or anonymous)

RSI Verification Data:
  • RSI handle and the verification code you provide when using the /register 
    command

Leveling and Progress Data:
  • XP points, levels, and earned roles

PVP Tracking Data:
  • Kill/loss statistics and related ship information

Server Logs:
  • Message edits, deletions, member joins/leaves, and other moderation-related 
    audit logs

We do not collect your Discord password, payment information, or any sensitive 
personal data beyond what is necessary for the Bot's features.

================================================================================

3. HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and operate Bot features (leveling, verification, tickets, feedback, 
    image generation, PVP tracking, etc.)
  • Maintain server moderation and security
  • Generate personalized content (e.g., banners and division cards using your 
    avatar and display name)
  • Improve the Bot and respond to support requests
  • Comply with legal obligations and prevent abuse

================================================================================

4. DATA SHARING

We do not sell your data to third parties.

We may share information only in the following cases:

  • With Discord, as required to operate the Bot via the Discord API
  • Public RSI profile lookups (one-time, voluntary) when you use the /register 
    command
  • With law enforcement or government authorities when required by law
  • To trusted staff members of The Coalition for moderation, support, and 
    server management purposes

================================================================================

5. DATA STORAGE AND SECURITY

Most Bot data is stored in secure local JSON files on our hosting server 
(e.g., leveling.json, data/pvp.json, userRegisterCodes.json).

Some data (tickets, logs, feedback) is also stored in Discord channels 
accessible only to authorized staff.

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data. 
However, no system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute 
security.

================================================================================

6. DATA RETENTION

We retain data only as long as necessary to provide the service or as required 
for legitimate purposes (e.g., audit logs for moderation). You may request 
deletion of your data at any time (see Section 8).

================================================================================

7. ANONYMOUS FEEDBACK

Anonymous feedback is designed to protect your identity from public view. 
However, we may still access metadata for abuse prevention, moderation, or 
legal compliance if the content violates our rules.

================================================================================

8. YOUR RIGHTS

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your data (XP, feedback, registration 
    info, PVP stats, etc.)
  • Object to or restrict certain processing of your data

To exercise these rights, please open a HelpDesk ticket in The Coalition 
Discord server or contact staff directly. We will respond to your request 
within a reasonable timeframe, subject to operational and legal constraints.

================================================================================

9. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

The Bot is not intended for children under 13 years of age. If you are under 
13, you may not use the Bot. We do not knowingly collect data from children 
under 13. If we become aware that we have collected information from a child 
under 13, we will delete it promptly.

================================================================================

10. CHANGES TO THIS PRIVACY POLICY

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be 
announced in the Discord server. Your continued use of the Bot after changes 
are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.

================================================================================

11. CONTACT US

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our data 
practices, please open a ticket via the HelpDesk panel or contact TCICORPS 
staff directly.
